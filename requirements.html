Online EMBA Program Admissions Requirements

Become Part of Our Legacy

The Howard University online Executive MBA is designed for both experienced and emerging business leaders who want to strengthen their skills for the executive level. Meeting the admissions requirements means you have the foundation, experience, and ability to succeed in this rigorous program. You are also assured that your fellow students, with whome you will network and collaborate, are of the select caliber that you expect.


Admission Requirements

  • Applicants must have a degree from a regionally accredited institution in order to qualify for the program
  • Cumulative GPA of 3.0 or higher recommended by not required
  • Minimum of seven years professional work experience, with five years in a management or senior leadership role
  • GMAT recommended, but not required (we allude to the senior leadership experience in the preceding requirement)

    • Submit for Application

    • Official transcripts from all undergraduate and graduate institutions attended
    • Statement of Interest
    • $75 non-refundable application fee
  • Current resume or CV detailing all previous work experience
  • Three letter of recommendation, one from a current supervisor

    • Foreign Students

    The Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) results for international students for whom English is not their native language must be forwarded from the Education Testing Service

  • Minimum scored required: Written Exam: 550 | Computer-based exam: 213

    • Transfer Credits

    No transfer credits are accepted for this program.
